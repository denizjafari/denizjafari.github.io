Consulting Services

Technology Entrepreneurship & Business Development

I have experience and passion in technology entrepreneurship, with a proven record in securing over $150,000 in government grants and loans for businesses. My background includes developing business models, providing feedback on scientific proposals and post-graduate NSERC applications, and serving for several years as a teaching assistant for the University of Toronto's undergraduate entrepreneurship certificate courses.

I offer consulting services in business plan development, grant and funding applications, and digital technology implementation for startups and small- to medium-sized businesses. If interested, feel free to reach out.
