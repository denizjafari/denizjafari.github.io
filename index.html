Welcome

Welcome to my personal website! I'm a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Toronto, passionate about developing innovative rehabilitation technologies and advancing healthcare solutions.

About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Toronto under the supervision of Dr. Alex Mihailidis. My work focuses on the design and development of a modular upper limb rehabilitation robot for post-stroke therapy. I hold an MSc in Rehabilitation Sciences, an MHSc in Clinical Engineering, and a BASc in Engineering Science (Aerospace) all from UofT.

Research Interests

I have a deep passion for engineering, system design, and development. My main research interests revolve around creating practical and affordable health care solutions that enhance quality of life globally. I love identifying gaps, defining requirements, and designing integrated systems that combine mechanical, electrical, and software elements. Ultimately, I focus on developing robotic, multimodal sensor-based technologies that interact smoothly with humans to optimize human-robot interaction and meet human needs.
