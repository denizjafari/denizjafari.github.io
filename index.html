About Me

"Unfiltered me, standing at the edge of the land, taking in the wonders of the world, where two seas meet and their waves collide in a mesmerizing dance of physics."

Pic taken in Skagen, Denmark

I am a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Toronto under the supervision of Dr. Alex Mihailidis. My work focuses on the design and development of a modular upper limb rehabilitation system for home basedpost-stroke therapy. I hold an MSc in Rehabilitation Sciences, an MHSc in Clinical Engineering, and a BASc in Engineering Science (Aerospace) all from UofT.
