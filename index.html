About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Toronto under the supervision of Dr. Alex Mihailidis. My work focuses on the design and development of a modular upper limb rehabilitation robot for post-stroke therapy. I hold an MSc in Rehabilitation Sciences and an MHSc in Clinical Engineering, both from UofT.

Research & Experience

  • PhD Researcher (Sept 2022 – Present): Developing a modular upper limb rehabilitation robot for customized stroke therapy.
  • MSc Researcher (Sept 2019 – Aug 2022): AI-based assessment of facial motor deficits in neurodegenerative diseases.
  • MHSc Researcher (Sept 2016 – Aug 2019): Navigation workflow for radiofrequency ablation of spinal metastases.
